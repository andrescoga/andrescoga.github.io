Pink Floyd

Post-Production Lead & Editor

I led post‑production across three major Pink Floyd catalog campaigns—The Dark Side of the Moon, Live at Pompeii – MCMLXXII, and Wish You Were Here—supporting global releases that spanned pre‑orders, theatrical screenings (including IMAX), and multi‑format reissues. My role focused on executing high‑volume deliverables under strict release timelines, ensuring visual consistency across platforms, territories, and formats. I cut trailers, developed campaign assets, and produced localized deliverables in multiple aspect ratios and languages for streaming services, social platforms, and theatrical promotion, helping support a campaign that culminated in a worldwide cinema run and a UK #1 album for Live at Pompeii.
