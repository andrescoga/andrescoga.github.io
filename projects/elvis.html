Elvis' 30 #1

Elvis Presley

Production & Post-Supervisor, Motion Designer

Managed production and post for Elvis Presley's thirty #1 singles — twenty-eight lyric videos spanning six decades of recordings. I built a visual system flexible enough to give each video its own identity while maintaining a consistent look across the series, then oversaw production and coordinated delivery. The visual language leaned on bold graphic typography and original Graceland archival footage.
