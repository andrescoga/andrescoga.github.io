Short Docs

The Making of The Dark Side of the Moon

Pink Floyd — Edit

Edited a short documentary tracing the creation of The Dark Side of the Moon. The challenge was shaping familiar archival material into something with a clear narrative spine — the specific decisions, conflicts, and technical breakthroughs that defined the record rather than the mythology around it.

Musicology 20th Anniversary

Prince — Edit

Cut a short retrospective marking the 20th anniversary of Prince's Musicology, working with archival footage and press material from the original release era. The piece captures what made the record unusual — Prince's return to a major label, his direct-to-fan distribution approach, and the tour that became one of the highest-grossing of that year.

Making of "That You Not Dare to Forget"

Miles Electric Band — Edit

Edited a behind-the-scenes documentary for the Miles Electric Band following the creation of their album. The piece moves between studio process and the musicians behind it, using performance footage and interviews to build a portrait of the project as it was being made.

20 Years of Laundry Service

Shakira — Edit

Cut a short anniversary piece marking two decades of Shakira's Laundry Service — the album that defined her crossover. The edit draws on original footage and press material to trace the album's global reach and its place in the pop landscape it helped reshape.
