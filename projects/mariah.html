Christmas Campaign

Mariah Carey

Content Producer & Editor

Christmas campaigns for Mariah Carey, one of the most enduring seasonal properties in music, spanning multiple holiday seasons across social, digital, and platform-specific content tied to All I Want for Christmas Is You and the broader Christmas catalog.

I produced content and coordinated delivery across formats and platforms, working closely with marketing, digital, and artist teams to keep everything aligned throughout the rollout.

The work includes a high volume of global assets, from social content and sizzle reels to vertical formats for digital and DSP integrations. The campaign was recognized with a Silver Award from the Clio Music Awards.
