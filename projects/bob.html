Why Does the Grim Reaper Exist

About this project

This project for The New Yorker explores the cultural significance and symbolism of the Grim Reaper figure throughout history. The video combines archival footage, animation, and new interviews to examine why this personification of death has persisted across different cultures and time periods.

The editing approach focuses on creating a thoughtful rhythm that balances the serious subject matter with engaging visual storytelling. By weaving together historical imagery, art history references, and contemporary perspectives, the piece offers viewers a comprehensive look at how societies have visualized and processed the concept of mortality.
