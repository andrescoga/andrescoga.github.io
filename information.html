I've spent over a decade at the intersection of post-production and systems thinking — starting as a video editor and motion designer, and gradually building tools to handle the parts of the process that don't scale.

At Sony Music I oversee content production for major catalog campaigns — Elvis Presley, Michael Jackson, Mariah Carey, Pink Floyd. The role is part creative, part operational: managing teams, timelines, and delivery while staying close to the work itself.

Most of the automation work I build comes from watching the same bottleneck happen twice. Forced-alignment for lyric timing, translation scripting inside After Effects, batch rendering from a tracklist — each one started as a frustration before it became a tool.

Based in New York City.
